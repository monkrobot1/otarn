# Economy & Meta-Progression: The Divine Flow

The economy of Otaran is split between the eternal power of the God (Meta) and the temporary manifestation of power within a run (Ephemeral).

---

## 1. The Two Forms of Faith

### Divine Spark (Meta-Currency)
- **Source:** Earned upon run completion or defeat.
- **Usage:** Spent at the **Diamond Throne** between runs.
- **Effects:**
  - Permanently unlock new **Axiom Talents** to be added to the global draft pool.
  - Permanently unlock or encounter new **Relics** in the world.
  - Permanently increase baseline proxy stats (Ascension).

### Ephemeral Faith (Run Currency)
- **Source:** Earned during a specific run through combat, events, and node traversal.
- **Usage:** Spent during the **Faith Draft** (start of run) or at **Sanctuaries** / **Shops**.
- **Effects:**
  - Activate unlocked Talents for the current run.
  - Purchase mid-run upgrades or healing.
  - Resurrect fallen proxies.

---

## 2. The Diamond Throne Unlock Costs

| Unlock Type | Divine Spark Cost | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Talent Tier 1** | 50 | Adds 1 Common Talent to the draft pool. |
| **Talent Tier 2** | 150 | Adds 1 Rare Talent to the draft pool. |
| **Talent Tier 3** | 400 | Adds 1 Epic Talent to the draft pool. |
| **Talent Tier 4** | 1000 | Adds 1 God-Tier Talent to the draft pool. |
| **Relic Node** | 300 | Adds a specific Relic to the global loot table. |

---

## 3. Proxy Ascension
Spending Divine Sparks on specific Proxy profiles permanently increases their baseline rarity (**Stars**).
- **1-Star to 2-Star:** Increases primary class stats by 20%.
- **Benefits:** Ensures late-game runs begin with significantly stronger base units.
