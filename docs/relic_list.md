# Relic Compendium: The 80 Artifacts of Otaran

Relics are permanent modifiers acquired during a run from Elite encounters, Bosses, or Events. A party can hold a maximum of 4 Relics.

---

## Sector 1: The Cold Void (Judgment)
1.  **The Rusting Crown:** Every time an enemy takes DoT damage, your team gains +2% Grace (stacking).
2.  **Cybernetic Heart:** Max HP is reduced by 50%, but all champions gain 100% Debuff Immunity (Spirit).
3.  **Void Prism:** Spiritual attacks that miss (Acumen failure) refund 100% of their MP cost.
4.  **Executioner's Axe:** Physical attacks against enemies below 20% HP deal triple damage.
5.  **Dark Matter Battery:** Start every combat with maximum MP.
6.  **Shadow Cloak:** The Backline is entirely immune to single-target Physical attacks.
7.  **Nanite Injector:** Champions heal for 5% of their Max HP whenever they apply a debuff.
8.  **Decaying Core:** Enemies start combat with 1 stack of Decay.
9.  **Singularity Engine:** Killing an enemy advances the killer's next turn by 50%.
10. **Tungsten Plating:** Armor (Physical Resistance) cannot be reduced by enemy skills.
11. **Rogue AI Chip:** Champions have a 10% chance to act twice in one turn.
12. **Harvester's Scythe:** 5% of all damage dealt is returned as Max HP growth for the rest of the run.
13. **Zero-Point Module:** Skills cost 0 MP, but cost 10% of current HP instead.
14. **Frozen Chronometer:** The first turn of combat lasts twice as long for the timeline.
15. **Synthetic Soul:** Null-Forged characters gain +50% Spirit.
16. **Ocular Implant:** Critical hits can no longer be dodged.
17. **Void-Glass Visor:** Reveals the exact HP numbers and timeline position of bosses.
18. **Scrap Magnet:** Earn 20% more Ephemeral Faith from all sources.
19. **Neutron Star Fragment:** Gravity/Movement effects applied by your team are twice as effective.
20. **The Arch-Magister's Seal:** Negates the first death of a run entirely, breaking this relic.

## Sector 2: The Astral Courts (Order)
1.  **Gavel of the High Priest:** The first attack of combat cannot be dodged and is a guaranteed Critical Strike.
2.  **Pendulum of Truth:** Turn order cannot be manipulated by enemies. Your timeline position is absolute.
3.  **Magnetic Tethers:** If slot 1 dies, slot 2 gains +200% Physicality for 3 turns.
4.  **Aegis Shield:** The entire party starts combat with a shield blocking 1 hit.
5.  **Hourglass of Solarius:** Once per combat, you can manually reset the timeline to the start.
6.  **Codex of Law:** Buffs applied to your team can never be dispelled.
7.  **Paladin's Gauntlet:** Healing spells also grant a physical shield equal to the heal amount.
8.  **Inquisitor's Branding Iron:** Enemies hit by Spiritual attacks lose 5% damage output permanently.
9.  **Gravity Boots:** Frontline champions gain +30% Grace.
10. **Chrono-Dial:** Delaying an enemy on the timeline damages them for 5% of their Max HP.
11. **Cathedral Keystone:** +50% Max HP to Lumina characters.
12. **Scales of Justice:** If the enemy has more units alive than you, your remaining units deal +50% damage.
13. **Templar's Halo:** Heals the lowest HP champion for 10% Max HP at the end of every turn.
14. **Magnetic Core:** Taunted enemies have their damage reduced by 25%.
15. **Truth-Seeker Lens:** Reveals hidden enemy weaknesses.
16. **Feather of Maat:** Critical strikes against your team deal regular damage instead.
17. **Sun-Forged Blade:** Physical attacks ignore 30% of enemy Armor.
18. **Orb of Absolute Zero:** Stuns the entire enemy team on Turn 1.
19. **Decree Scroll:** Your first skill used in combat costs 0 MP.
20. **The Pontiff's Crown:** Grants +1 Level to all champions permanently.

## Sector 3: The Wild Maelstrom (Chaos)
1.  **Bottle of Maelstrom:** Randomizes all enemy stats at the start of combat.
2.  **Cinderstone:** Burn effects now reduce enemy Physical Resistance by 5% per tick.
3.  **Zephyr Wings:** Every time a champion Dodges (Fate), their next attack deals double damage.
4.  **Geode Heart:** Earth spells grant the caster +20% Physicality for 2 turns.
5.  **Tidecaller's Conch:** Water spells cleanse ALL debuffs from the target.
6.  **Obsidian Shard:** Physical damage taken is capped at 15% of a champion's Max HP per hit.
7.  **Ignis-Tal's Ember:** Burn damage scales infinitely the longer combat lasts.
8.  **Storm-Caller Wand:** Multi-hit attacks have a 25% chance to hit a secondary target.
9.  **Primal Drum:** Every 3rd attack your team makes is a guaranteed Critical Strike.
10. **Elemental Compass:** Discover all map nodes automatically in Sector 3.
11. **Volatile Flask:** 10% chance for any attack to deal 400% damage, 10% chance to heal the enemy instead.
12. **Lava Stone:** Frontline champions reflect 20% of melee damage as Fire damage.
13. **Wind-Chime of the Fae:** Dodge (Fate) chance is doubled against Bosses.
14. **Tsunami Pearl:** Healing spells bounce to a second ally for 50% effectiveness.
15. **Earthquake Hammer:** Crits have a 20% chance to Stun the target.
16. **Salamander Scale:** Immune to Burn effects.
17. **Sylph's Breath:** +50% Acumen to Espers.
18. **Djinn's Lamp:** Grants 3 free Talent drafts at the next Sanctuary.
19. **Meteorite Fragment:** The first Spiritual Attack of combat hits ALL enemies.
20. **The Sovereign's Eye:** Randomly transforms one regular enemy into a loot-bearing Elite at the start of combat.

## Sector 4: The Verdant Cradle (Love)
1.  **Vampiric Seed:** 10% of all Physical Damage dealt by your team is returned as HP to the lowest health champion.
2.  **Dreamcatcher:** Enemies that wake up from Sleep suffer massive Spiritual damage based on your team's total Authority.
3.  **Spore Cloud Generator:** Whenever a champion dies, the rest of the team is healed for 100% of the dead champion's Max HP.
4.  **World-Tree Branch:** All champions gain +1 HP regeneration per turn for every 10 Max HP they have.
5.  **Blood-Chalice:** HP sacrifices for skills cost 50% less.
6.  **Sylvan Heart:** Healing spells can critically heal based on the Acumen stat.
7.  **Mycelium Network:** Buffs applied to one champion spread to all adjacent champions at 50% strength.
8.  **Ethereal Bloom:** Revived champions come back with full MP.
9.  **Mother-Spore's Tear:** Grants complete immunity to Sleep and Stun effects.
10. **Carnivorous Plant Bulb:** Enemies below 10% HP are instantly eaten and killed at the start of their turn.
11. **Flesh-Shaper's Scalpel:** Healing an ally also increases their Authority by 5% for the rest of combat.
12. **Lotus Blossom:** Reduces the MP cost of all skills by 2.
13. **Root-Bound Anklet:** Enemies cannot dodge while afflicted by a debuff.
14. **Symbiotic Parasite:** Equip to a specific champion; they gain +100% stats but die instantly after 3 combats.
15. **Dream-Walker's Staff:** Sleep effects applied by your team never break from taking damage.
16. **Chrysalis:** If a champion is killed, they enter a cocoon. If the cocoon survives 2 turns, they revive with full HP.
17. **Bioluminescent Lantern:** Reduces enemy Hit Chance (Grace) by 15%.
18. **Sanguine Rose:** Every critical strike heals the attacker for 5% Max HP.
19. **Elyria's Blessing:** Gain 1 extra champion slot (5v4 combat) for standard encounters (disables during boss fights).
20. **The Seed of Otaran:** Generates 50 Ephemeral Faith at the start of every node.
