# Boss Compendium: The Usurpers

Each sector is ruled by a former disciple of the God who betrayed them. Conquering a sector requires defeating the High Priest, but the path is guarded by specialized Sub-Bosses.

---

## Sector 1 (Judgment): Arch-Magister Nihilus
**Backstory:** Once the God's chief adjudicator, Nihilus replaced his own soul with a micro-singularity, becoming a cold, unfeeling void.

### Sub-Bosses:
- **The Iron Judge:** Physical execution protocol.
- **Whisper-Net Prime:** Evasive assassin and data-thief.
- **The Flesh Harvester:** Drain tank that sustains itself on the remnants of flesh.
- **Reactor 0-Decay:** Stacking DoT specialist focused on entropic breakdown.

---

## Sector 2 (Order): Pontiff-Imperator Solarius
**Backstory:** Solarius seized control of time and gravity to freeze his sector in a state of eternal, unchanging "peace."

### Sub-Bosses:
- **Grand Marshal Aegis:** Heavy defense and damage reflection.
- **The Chrono-Inquisitor:** Manipulates time to delete player turns.
- **Lord Gravitas:** Initiative crusher using high-gravity wells.
- **The Oracle of Truth:** Buff dispeller and absolute logic-weaver.

---

## Sector 3 (Chaos): The Maelstrom Sovereign, Ignis-Tal
**Backstory:** Drank directly from planetary leylines, shattering his mind into a volatile being of pure elemental rage.

### Sub-Bosses:
- **The Cinder-King:** Ramping fire damage and heat-strokes.
- **Deep-Trench Leviathan:** Timeline manipulation and crushing pressure.
- **The Obsidian Colossus:** Physical immunity and crushing weight.
- **The Howling Gale:** Massive dodge and wind-based retaliation.

---

## Sector 4 (Love): Mother-Spore Elyria
**Backstory:** Her desire to protect life grew into a twisted obsession to merge all biological beings into a single psychic hive-mind.

### Sub-Bosses:
- **The Sanguine Root:** Sacrificial nukes and blood-bonded servants.
- **The Dream-Weaver:** Sleep assassin that strikes from the subconscious.
- **Overgrowth Behemoth:** Over-heal scaling and massive organic bulk.
- **Chimera of Life:** Multi-phase revival and shifting biological traits.
