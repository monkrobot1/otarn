# Lore & Worldbuilding: Awakening of Otaran

## 1. High-Level Concept
Awakening of Otaran is a journey of a forgotten god, awakened after 10,000 years to find their domain usurped. Operating from the **Diamond Throne**, the player recruits and commands mortal proxies to conquer four distinct planetary sectors, each ruled by a traitorous High Priest representing a foundational Axiom of reality.

### Visual Aesthetic: "The Space God Interface"
The game's visual identity fuses dreamy symbolism with clean sci-fi lines.
- **Environments:** Swirling, impressionistic cosmic backgrounds, nebulae, and surreal planetary landscapes.
- **UI/UX:** Crisp, minimalist, ultra-modern sci-fi interfaces. Thin white/gold geometric lines, glassmorphism, and sterile, readable typography.

## 2. Sectors & Races
The humans of Otaran have been forcefully evolved or mutated under the rule of the High Priests into four distinct races.

### Sector 1: The Cold Void (Judgment)
- **Axiom:** Absence
- **Theme:** Cyborg Sci-Fi. Chilling, brutalist aesthetic defined by cold steel, cybernetic enhancements, and inevitable entropy.
- **Dominant Race: The Null-Forged.** Humans who abandoned flesh. Androids, cyborgs, and synthetics driven by cold logic and dark matter.
- **Traits:** High base Physicality and Capacity, low Grace.

### Sector 2: The Astral Courts (Order)
- **Axiom:** Law
- **Theme:** Medieval in Space. Techno-feudalism featuring laser swords, energy shields, grand cosmic cathedrals, and strict, absolute hierarchies.
- **Dominant Race: The Lumina.** Genetically perfected "Numans" engineered for rigid societal roles. Knightly and bound by honor.
- **Traits:** High base Grace and Spirit.

### Sector 3: The Wild Maelstrom (Chaos)
- **Axiom:** Primal
- **Theme:** Dangerous Elemental Whimsy. A vibrant, volatile, highly magical aesthetic where raw elements crash together.
- **Dominant Race: The Espers (Primordials).** Humans heavily mutated by planetary anomalies, evolving into Fae-like elementals.
- **Traits:** High base Authority and Acumen, low Physicality.

### Sector 4: The Verdant Cradle (Love)
- **Axiom:** Creation
- **Theme:** Benevolent Nature. Lush, bio-luminescent, overgrown environments focused on organic technology and symbiosis.
- **Dominant Race: The Sylvan.** Humans who formed symbiotic bonds with flora, spores, and world-trees. Flesh-shaping and ethereal.
- **Traits:** High base Fate and Capacity.
