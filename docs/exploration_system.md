# Exploration & Navigation: Branching the Void

The core gameplay loop between combat is built upon a branching, FTL-style node map representing planetary systems within a High Priest's sector.

## Navigation Rules
- **Forward Only:** The player must continuously move from left to right across the cosmic web. You cannot backtrack.
- **Branching Paths:** Nodes frequently split, forcing the player to choose between high-risk/high-reward paths or safer paths.
- **Node Consumption:** Once a node is visited, it is considered "depleted," and the player must advance to one of the connected forward nodes.

## Pre-cognition & Node Previews
Because the player is a God operating from the Diamond Throne, they possess "Pre-cognition."
- **Sensory Preview:** Hovering over an upcoming node gives a vague sensory preview of what lies ahead.
- **Destiny Scaling:** The clarity and detail of this preview scale based on the party's total **Destiny** stat.
  - *Low Destiny:* Only the node type (Combat, Event, etc.) is visible.
  - *High Destiny:* Specific enemy types, potential rewards, or event outcomes may be revealed before committing to the path.

## Node Types
- **Combat:** Standard encounter with Sector-specific enemies.
- **Elite:** A much tougher encounter with a guaranteed Relic reward.
- **Boss:** The sector's High Priest or a major gatekeeper.
- **Event:** A narrative encounter with choices and branching outcomes.
- **Sanctuary:** A place to rest, heal, and potentially revive champions using Ephemeral Faith.
- **Shop:** A rare node where Relics or Axiom Talents can be purchased directly.
