# Axiom Talents: The 120 Doctrines

At the start of a run, the player receives a randomized pool of Talents. They spend Ephemeral Faith to lock them in.

---

## Axiom of Absence (Judgment)
**Focus:** Execute, Debuffs, Armor

### Tier 1 (Common) - Cost: 10 Faith
1.  **Cold Logic:** +10% base Physicality to all Null-Forged.
2.  **Void Stare:** +5% Authority to Backline champions.
3.  **Rusty Joints:** Enemies start combat with -5% Grace.
4.  **Metallic Plating:** +5% Physicality (Armor) to Frontline champions.
5.  **Calculated Strikes:** +5% Acumen to all champions.
6.  **Dark Matter Reserves:** +10% Capacity (MP) to all Null-Forged.
7.  **Inevitability:** Decay effects deal +10% damage.
8.  **Hollow Frame:** +5% Fate (Dodge) against Physical Attacks.
9.  **Sensor Calibration:** +5% Grace (Hit Chance) against high-Dodge enemies.
10. **Coolant Leak:** Enemies hitting your Frontline lose 2% Grace.
11. **Executioner's Eye:** +10% Critical Multiplier against enemies below 50% HP.
12. **Synthetic Blood:** Bleed effects deal 50% less damage to Null-Forged.

### Tier 2 (Rare) - Cost: 25 Faith
13. **Entropy Protocol:** DoT (Decay) effects last 1 additional turn.
14. **Synthetic Armor:** Frontline champions ignore the first physical attack of the battle.
15. **Dark Matter Core:** Killing an enemy grants +10 MP (Capacity) to the killer.
16. **Shadow-Step:** Backline champions gain +20% Fate (Dodge) on the first turn.
17. **Reaper's Toll:** Physical attacks heal the user for 5% of the damage dealt to enemies with Decay.
18. **Overclocked Actuators:** Null-Forged gain +10% Grace when below 30% HP.
19. **Null-Zone:** The first Spiritual Attack from an enemy is automatically resisted.
20. **Vulture Protocol:** When an ally dies, all living Null-Forged gain +25% Physicality for 2 turns.

### Tier 3 (Epic) - Cost: 50 Faith
21. **Reanimator's Grace:** The first time a champion dies, resurrect them with 25% HP.
22. **Zero Kelvin:** Crits applied to enemies afflicted by a debuff deal +50% Acumen multiplier.
23. **Singularity Drive:** Enemies killed by Void-Borgs cannot trigger on-death effects.
24. **Absolute Decay:** Decay now also reduces enemy Physicality (Armor) by 10% per tick.
25. **Ghost in the Machine:** Null-Forged cannot be critically hit.
26. **The Flesh is Weak:** All healing received is reduced by 50%, but base Physicality is doubled.

### Tier 4 (God-Tier) - Cost: 100 Faith
27. **Absolute Zero:** Once per combat, instantly execute any non-boss enemy below 30% HP.
28. **Heat Death:** All DoT effects deal damage at the start AND end of a turn.
29. **Event Horizon:** Enemies that miss an attack lose their next turn entirely.
30. **The Great Silence:** For the first 2 turns of combat, enemies cannot use Spiritual (Magic) attacks.

## Axiom of Law (Order)
**Focus:** Speed, Shields, Pure Damage

### Tier 1 (Common) - Cost: 10 Faith
1.  **Rigid Doctrine:** +10% base Spirit to all Lumina.
2.  **Clockwork Steps:** +5% Grace to all champions.
3.  **Heavy Gravity:** Enemy Dodge (Fate) reduced by 10%.
4.  **Shield Wall:** +10% Physical Resistance to Frontline.
5.  **True Aim:** +5% Spiritual Hit Chance (Acumen) to all champions.
6.  **Magnetic Pull:** Taunt effects last 1 additional turn.
7.  **Honor Bound:** Champions deal +5% damage to full-health enemies.
8.  **Dogmatic Will:** +10% Debuff Resistance to all Lumina.
9.  **Weighted Dice:** Fate (Crit Chance) increased by 5%.
10. **Vanguard's Light:** Frontline champions gain +5% max HP.
11. **Inquisitor's Zeal:** +5% Authority against shielded enemies.
12. **Temporal Echo:** +5% chance to retain MP when casting a skill.

### Tier 2 (Rare) - Cost: 25 Faith
13. **Magnetic Field:** Frontline shares 20% of damage taken with the backline.
14. **Absolute Truth:** Attacks that hit automatically bypass 10% of enemy Physicality/Spirit.
15. **Chrono-Lock:** Enemies cannot act before your fastest champion on the first turn.
16. **Grav-Well:** Enemies delayed on the timeline take 10% more damage from the next attack.
17. **Paladin's Oath:** Healing a champion to full HP grants them a 10% Physicality shield.
18. **Stalwart Defense:** Champions with a shield cannot be critically hit.
19. **Momentum:** Every time a champion acts, their Grace increases by 2% (stacking).
20. **Verdict:** Hitting an enemy with 3 different champions in one round inflicts Stun.

### Tier 3 (Epic) - Cost: 50 Faith
21. **Chrono-Shift:** Moving a unit back on the timeline also heals them for 10% max HP.
22. **Impenetrable Aegis:** Shields absorb Spiritual damage as well as Physical damage.
23. **The Scales Tip:** If an ally's HP falls below 20%, their next attack deals True (Unmitigable) damage.
24. **Perfect Symmetry:** If the Frontline and Backline have the exact same combined HP percentages, all stats are boosted by 15%.
25. **Magnetic Resonance:** When a shield breaks, it deals its value as Spiritual damage to the attacker.
26. **Time-Dilation:** Your champions' buffs last 1 extra turn; enemy debuffs last 1 extra turn.

### Tier 4 (God-Tier) - Cost: 100 Faith
27. **The Grand Cathedral:** Start every battle with a shield on all champions equal to 50% of their Max HP.
28. **Absolute Decree:** Once per combat, instantly strip all buffs from all enemies and all debuffs from all allies.
29. **Singularity:** All damage dealt by your team is calculated against the enemy's lowest defensive stat.
30. **Rewind:** Once per run, if your party wipes, restart the battle from Turn 1 with full HP.

## Axiom of Primal (Chaos)
**Focus:** Crit, Multi-hits, Elements

### Tier 1 (Common) - Cost: 10 Faith
1.  **Volatile Spark:** +10% base Acumen to all Espers.
2.  **Shifting Winds:** +5% Dodge (Fate) to all champions.
3.  **Sparking Embers:** +5% Authority to all champions.
4.  **Fluid Stance:** +5% Grace after dodging an attack.
5.  **Earthen Core:** +10% Physicality to Geomancers.
6.  **Kindling:** Burn damage increased by 10%.
7.  **Refreshing Waters:** Healing skills cost 10% less MP.
8.  **Static Charge:** +5% Critical Strike Chance.
9.  **Unpredictable Nature:** +10% Destiny (Meta-stat for events).
10. **Whirlwind:** Multi-hit skills have a 5% chance to add one extra strike.
11. **Magma Veins:** Espers gain +5% max HP.
12. **Thermal Shock:** Enemies afflicted by Burn have -5% Spirit.

### Tier 2 (Rare) - Cost: 25 Faith
13. **Wild Magic:** Spiritual attacks have a 20% chance to cast twice.
14. **Scorched Earth:** Enemies that attack a frontline champion suffer Burn.
15. **Riptide:** Healing a target also advances their position on the timeline by 5%.
16. **Stone Skin:** The first physical attack taken by each champion is reduced by 50%.
17. **Gale Force:** Dodging an attack grants +20% Acumen to the next Spiritual attack.
18. **Chain Lightning:** Critical hits splash 20% of their damage to a random adjacent enemy.
19. **Volcanic Eruption:** When an Esper dies, they deal massive Fire damage to all enemies.
20. **Unstable Elements:** Every turn, one random enemy loses 20% Spirit.

### Tier 3 (Epic) - Cost: 50 Faith
21. **Elemental Roulette:** Every turn, one random champion gains +100% Damage for that turn only.
22. **Geothermic Core:** Burn effects now heal your Frontline for 50% of the damage dealt.
23. **Tidal Wave:** Cleansing a debuff from an ally heals the entire party for 15% Max HP.
24. **Eye of the Storm:** If a champion dodges 3 attacks in one combat, they gain 100% Crit Chance for 2 turns.
25. **Primal Resonance:** Having all four elements (Fire, Earth, Water, Air) in your party grants +20% to all stats.
26. **Wildfire:** Burn effects spread to one random enemy at the end of each turn.

### Tier 4 (God-Tier) - Cost: 100 Faith
27. **Primal Big Bang:** Crits now hit all enemies for 50% of the damage dealt.
28. **Avatar of Chaos:** At the start of combat, one random champion becomes immune to all damage for 3 turns.
29. **Maelstrom Unleashed:** All multi-hit skills are guaranteed to hit their maximum number of strikes.
30. **World Breaker:** Spiritual Attacks ignore 100% of enemy Spirit (Resistance).

## Axiom of Creation (Love)
**Focus:** Healing, Max HP, Buffs

### Tier 1 (Common) - Cost: 10 Faith
1.  **Deep Roots:** +10% base HP (Capacity) to all Sylvan.
2.  **Benevolence:** Healing effects increased by 15%.
3.  **Spore Cloud:** +5% Debuff Resist to all champions.
4.  **Photosynthesis:** Champions heal 2% Max HP at the start of their turn.
5.  **Blood Tithe:** +5% Physicality to Blood-Druids.
6.  **Dreamers' Haze:** +5% Acumen to Backline champions.
7.  **Vitality Spores:** Max HP increased by 5% for all champions.
8.  **Empathy:** When the Frontline takes damage, the Backline gains +2% Authority.
9.  **Thick Bark:** +5% Physical Resistance to Sylvan.
10. **Lullaby:** Sleep effects are 10% more likely to land.
11. **Growth Spurt:** Buffs applied by your team are 10% stronger.
12. **Symbiont:** +5% Fate (Crit Chance) for Sylvan.

### Tier 2 (Rare) - Cost: 25 Faith
13. **Symbiotic Link:** When a champion is healed, the healer gains 50% of that healing.
14. **Dream State:** Sleep effects applied to enemies last an extra turn and lower their Spirit.
15. **Blood Magic:** Skills that cost HP to cast deal 20% more damage.
16. **Verdant Shield:** Over-healing is converted into a temporary physical shield.
17. **Nurturing Touch:** Resurrected champions return with 50% HP instead of the default amount.
18. **Psychic Web:** If one champion dodges an attack, the whole team gains +10% Dodge for 1 turn.
19. **Thorn Armor:** Enemies attacking with Physical attacks take 10% of the damage back.
20. **Flesh Mend:** Cleansing a debuff restores 10% of the target's Max HP.

### Tier 3 (Epic) - Cost: 50 Faith
21. **Overgrowth:** Healing a champion past max HP converts the excess into permanent Physicality for that combat.
22. **Sanguine Harvest:** Killing an enemy restores 10% HP to the entire party.
23. **Lucid Dreaming:** Enemies asleep take 50% more damage from the attack that wakes them up.
24. **World-Tree Roots:** Frontline champions cannot be moved or delayed on the timeline by enemies.
25. **Flesh Sculptor:** When a Sylvan drops below 20% HP, their stats are completely randomized (can only increase).
26. **Blossom:** At the start of Turn 4, all dead champions are revived with 10% HP.

### Tier 4 (God-Tier) - Cost: 100 Faith
27. **The World Tree:** All champions regenerate 15% of their Max HP at the start of their turn.
28. **Unconditional Love:** Any attack that would kill a champion leaves them at 1 HP instead (Once per champion per combat).
29. **Genesis:** Max HP of all champions is doubled, but damage output is reduced by 20%.
30. **Hive Mind:** All damage taken is distributed evenly among all four champions.
